Project 6: Data Portrait

Description: This project visualizes data that had been collected over the course of a week's time without using bar graphs or pie charts. The collected data is the amount of music I listen to based on the day of the week. The more green you see, the more music I listened to on that particular day of the week. You may switch between different days by clicking on the canvas.
